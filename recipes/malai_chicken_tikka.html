Malai Chicken Tikka

Description

Murgh Malai tikka is an Indian chicken dish where marinated chicken pieces are grilled over charcoal. It means chicken pieces marinated in a spicy cream-based mixture.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoon oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 4 teaspoons ground cumin, divided
  • 1 tablespoon dried Fenugreek leaves
  • 1 cup Low-fat shredded mozzarrella cheese
  • 1 cup fresh cream
  • 1 cup cilantro leaves
  • 2 teaspoons salt, divided, or more to taste

Steps

  • Step 1 - Tenderize the meat

    • Thoroughly rinse the chicken. Pat the chicken dry with paper towels. Since we are making tikkas here, cut the chicken into 1-inch chicken cubes. Transfer the chicken to a bowl.Add salt, Fresh lemon juice.Toss it well. Allow the chicken to marinate for 30 minutes. This marinade gives the chicken a succulent bite.

  • Step 2 - Marinade 1

    • Meanwhile, let's prepare the first set of marinate. In a large mixing bowl, add cashew paste. Stir in ginger-garlic paste, green cardamom powder, and cornstarch. Add green chilies, salt, cumin powder, red chili powder, and garam masala powder. Mix until blended.

  • Step 3 - Marinade 2

    • Add yogurt, Cheese, kasoori methi, cream, cilantro leaves, and oil. Mix to combine.

  • Step 4 - Cooking

    • Add the chicken pieces. Mix to coat the murgh pieces evenly in the malai marinade. Cover and let the chicken marinate overnight in the refrigerator. Once the resting time is over, preheat the oven 400 degrees F. Remove chicken tikka from the fridge and thread the pieces onto the skewers. Evenly divide the cubes between 6 skewers. Cook the murgh malai tikka for 25-30 minutes, turning halfway through to cook evenly. Remove from the oven. Generously brush with butter and serve hot.

There you have it! Your Chicken Malai Tikka is ready!
